Purpose

Improving the quality of life of vulnerable pregnant women and their children

Mission

  1. To improve access to antenatal care for the less privileged expectant mothers
  2. To facilitate economic empowerment of mothers so that they can be able to provide for their children
  3. To provide counseling services to less privileged expectant mothers
  4. To provide new born essentials to less privileged expectant mothers

  5. transparency

Our Work

  1. Antenatal Care

    2. Women die because of pregnancy and childbirth complications. Most of these complications develop during pregnancy whilst others exist before pregnancy but are worsened during pregnancy especially if they are not managed well. All pregnant women need access to high quality care from skilled health personnel in pregnancy and after childbirth since health care solutions to prevent or manage complications are well known and timely management and treatment can make the difference between life and death for the mother as well as for the baby.

    Mother’s comfort provides antenatal registration fees to underprivileged mothers so they can have access to antenatal care.

    We also provide baby essentials (preparation)

  2. Counseling

    3. A happy mother equals a happy baby

    Pregnancy and parenthood can be a very difficult, hence maternal mental health should not be ignored. Maternal counselling improves maternal understanding and health practices, improves the well-being of the mother and the baby, and increases confidence in parenting.

    Mother’s Comfort provides counselling services to underprivileged pregnant women for their wellbeing and that of their children.

  3. Empowerment

    4. Economically empowered women tend to have healthier and more educated families. Mother’s Comfort provides skills training in tailoring, baking, poultry, agriculture, detergent making so that women gain skills to start working in these professions.

Mother's Comfort

We aim to improve the quality of life of vulnerable pregnant mothers and their children by helping them have acess to antenatal care, eduacting them of the importance of antenatal care, and reminding them of important matters concerning antenatal care. We also provide them with bay essentials and help the mothers to provide for their babies by empowering them with practical, marketable life skilss. We also offer counselling services to ensure that the mothers are in a good state of mind.

Mother's Comfort

Mother's comfort aims to improve the quality of life of pregnant women by offering them access to vital antenatal care. This is achieved through payment of antenatal registration fees, education on the need of early antenatal care,, sending antenatal care reminders and outreach programs. Offering essentials for the newborn babes, empowering mothers economically , and offering counselling services for the mother's mental well being.
